ABOUT ME

Hello there! My name is Khadir Williams and I am an aspiring Computer Scientist finishing up my bachelor's degree at the New Jersey Institue of Technology. My journey in Computer Science began in highschool in web and desktop publishing elective. In that class, I was taught the very basics of web development through HTML and CSS. I was fascinated at how HTML tags and CSS stylings could produce a webpage. I think of it as my first look under the hood of the car of web development. I knew from class that HTML and CSS are the engine that drives the development of web pages, but I wanted to be able to make my own car, and not just tinker around with the little knowledge I had of the engine. After that experience, I commited to three years of Computer Science in my high school's Computer Science elective program. In those years I was exposed to Python, Java, the Alice environment, Visual Basic, and Android App Development using AppInventor. My experiences with Computer Science in high school taught me a lot about myself and my passions. I love to create! Whether it be software, a website, a group project, or just a code snippet that contributes to a larger project, I take a lot pride and joy in my work and love watching it motion. Here on this site, I hope to show off some of my work and passion to the world.
